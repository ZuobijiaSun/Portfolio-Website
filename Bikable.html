Bikable

Overview

Fall 2021 | 2 Months | Bike-sharing

Bikable is a responsive platform for the bike-sharing program that gives users easier and safer access to bikes sharing networks to helps people locate and use shared-bikes nearby with personalization service and new technologies.

In the meantime, it also integrates the tracking statistics of fitness and environmental sustainability such as calories users burned or time and CO2 users saved with rewards as motivation in order to encourage more bike rides.
