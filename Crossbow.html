Crossbow

Overview

Fall 2019 | 2 Months | Device for the Disabled

This is a device I designed for people who have trouble with throwing stikcs far to play with their pets. It can help users to replease sticks without much strength.

Theoretically, crossbow is a bow mounted on an elongated frame with a built-in mechanism that holds the drawn bow string, as well as a triggre mechanism tat allows the string to be released. Based on this idea, I designed and built this device, but use sticks instead of bolts.
